4 Ways to Log

Pick the method that fits the situation. All entries are withdrawals.

Cash
Giving cash to a resident. Requires their signature on the receipt.
/start → THL → Bonnie → Cash → 20.00
Spend
Staff bought something for a resident. Describe what it was.
/start → THL → Bonnie → Spend → 12.50 → McDonalds
Text Dump
Quick entry. Just type the name, amount, and what it was. No buttons needed.
bonnie 12.50 mcdonalds lunch
Receipt Photo
Send a photo of the receipt with the resident name as caption. Auto-links to last transaction.
Send photo + caption: Bonnie

Rules

✍
Cash = Signature
Every cash withdrawal requires the resident's signature on the receipt. No exceptions.
📷
Keep Receipts
Send photos of store receipts to the bot with the resident's name. They auto-link to the transaction.
💰
Check Balance First
The bot shows the balance before any withdrawal. If it's low, notify Anthony.
🔒
Their Money
P&I funds belong to the resident. Every dollar must be accounted for and spent on them.

Log it when it happens.

Don't wait until end of shift. The bot takes 10 seconds. If you forget, the balance will be wrong and we can't fix it.
